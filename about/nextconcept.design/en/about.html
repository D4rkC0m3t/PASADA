How we do it

our process

We start with a detailed understanding of your needs, preferences and budget. We will take the time to listen to your ideas and vision, and work with you to create a design plan that reflects your unique style and personality.

projects

MEASUREMENTS ON SITE

The first stage of any successful project is to collect accurate information about the space. This is a crucial step in the design process as it allows us to understand the characteristics and limitations of the space and create a design that suits your needs and preferences.

QUOTATION / 3D RENDERING

After the first stage of the project is completed, we will prepare a customised price quote based on the information collected from the measurements. In addition, if you have requested, we will also provide you with photo-realistic renderings to help you imagine what the final space will look like.

DETAILED FINAL PROJECT

After the contract is signed, our team of designers and planners will start working on your project. At this stage, communication with you is very important to ensure that the project will be completed on time and that you will be completely satisfied with the result final.

INSTALLATION

The last step in our process is the installation of the furniture. Our team of skilled craftsmen will fit each piece of furniture into its intended location with precision and professionalism.
